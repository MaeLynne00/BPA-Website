Walt Disney World Park

The Walt Disney World Parks are a top tourist attraction. Nothing can beat the magic at the disney parks.At the Walt Disney World Resort you will find Magic Kingdom Park, Epcot, Disney’s Hollywood Studios, and Disney’s Animal Kingdom. Disney’s Blizzard Beach and Disney’s Typhoon Lagoon water parks are also open during the summer months.

Universal Theme Park

If you have ever wanted to take a step into any of your favorite TV shows or movies in real life then this is the place to go! At the Universal Theme Parks there are plenty of rides, dragons, live shows, and much more! This is beyond the movies.

Sea World

SeaWorld is known for its popular marine features such as dolphins, killer whales, sea lions, and also their live shows and rides. SeaWorld is one of the world’s foremost zoological organizations.
