Dress Code

All memebers attending the the conference must abide by the BPA dress code. Failure to comply to the dress code will result in the exclusion from events and sessions. It is recommended for any guests to abide by the established dress code.



Consent Form

All participants under the age of 18 are required to have a parental consent form on file. If they do not have it on file they will not have their scores released. The parental consent form can be downloaded here.
